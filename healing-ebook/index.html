— ii —

So, HOW does an abusive relationship feel? The dark, 'he hit me and it felt like a kiss' psysop.

Abusers take us away from the illusion of a normal life. U two may seem normal, but what u experience day to day is not. A new reality is warped, and that — minus all the negatives, is so reliving you would pay good money to experience it. The escape due to a trauma bond is a sober way to be on drugs.

Once you get to know this man, sometimes he feels so weak. Childlike. He is also at risk. He is tired from masking and changing personalities to charm & hide. He doesn't seem to have any control over his actions. Many have problems with authority & are already doing other illegal things, at risk of ending up in a cell, or struggling with his outbursts & drama that follows. He'll need you to protect him. you'll probably become his mother, a teacher, a true friend at many times during this. It's an extremely strange bond.

The verbal abuse makes u feel strong mentally. Breaks you down, breaks you open, you start to question everything.

— iii —

You start to trust him, he tells you things no one else would ever say. Now, no one else can hurt you. It feels like he actually does so much shadow work for you, it's so enlightening, you thank him for showing you how you need to grow.

Physical abuse, with enough manipulation, feels like tough love but mostly punishment. u can feel it changing ur body chemistry, feel fear & emotions u never felt before. The adrenaline and then the calm that comes after bonds you like animals. This is an evil way to get to our hearts because truly we:

Want to be re-wilded. We want to be wild. Wildness feels natural to us. We miss the animals we used to be.

We miss our power, we hate fakeness, we need a new path. And we can achieve all of ths in. fucking healthy way. without this loser.

In this relationship, U feel invincible but then ur body starts to show signs of hurt. ur brain hurts and u 'need' him. he becomes ur parent and ur god…

— iv —

and u become a shell of yourself. he's insanely good at repairing the damage, cos this was prolly all part of the plan <33

I think girls who get into these situations are usually such curious, sensitive people, open to intense energy. we crave a fight. Not a physical one. But a mission, for our lives to have purpose. We aren't perfect, no one is. But we crave meaning. Freedom, adrenaline.

We have to learn to redirect this risk, romance, obsession, to something new, healthy, Healthy ish. Us girls want danger because we ourselves are powerful beings who are really just complicated and starved of real emotion. And the way you do this will be extremely tailored to you. you must be patient. You must allow the process.

You must be brave, have faith in a new future, and be so delusional about it working out. even if u wanna die rn, you need this vision for future you to keep u going. u might not understand it yet. even if its just a feeling, hold on tight to that.
— v —

Where u will end up if you stay, or go back.

A genuine warning, because everyone tells you and you don't listen, It is irritating to hear. It becomes repetitive but u also have cognitive dissonance. You know u are not safe but YOU are alchemising and transforming…

Sometimes u just need to find out for yourself, reach your new low, high, come to your own conclusion. You decide. Ur beyond ready to give yourself ONE chance. instead of him his 20th. If you don't you might end up dead or dead inside. He won't ever change, in fact it will, and it aways, gets worse. It's not romantic.

Okayyy? YES <3 so

Separate ur feelings, chemistry, your idea of reality and look at this for what it is. ur under a spell that feels like love and truth, but right now is hurting you.

— vi —

*im in pain but this is my soul mate, if we get separated I am nothing*… a clear indication you have been successfully manipulated and ur being used.

Because you've repressed ur intuition, all reasoning, you cannot trust yourself right now. THE MIND FUCK IS UNREAL. let's acknowledge that. THIS PART IS HARD, the next is worse, but it's all part processing.

Once it's over, u do not know who u are anymore. the depersonalisation, dissociation, the death of your old self, reality warping back to 'normal'. The days after r so scarily quiet, so wrong, u trust no one. I missed the chaos, sympathy felt dirty, I held onto memories, videos, pics of the injuries. But I hated the word victim so much. I felt like I was equally as bad, just not illegally.

I wanted to keep this energy of evil and excitement forever. Worried that if it would fade, my spark would go out, the connection would mean nothing, no one seeing what I went through, how strong I was, how boring and slow healing felt.

— vii —

You'll have a period of mixed feelings, serious loneliness, feeling like it was your fault, you could have saved him, feeling like an evil monster. accepting you aren't a good person. But Girl rn you are essentially going through a withdrawal that is chemically stronger than one off drugs, your nervous system is resetting and your mind has yet to catch up. this healing I call 'glitching'

OK. NOW?

All your pain is yours and yours to hold. What if this happened, not just to you, but for you. Now you get to take full accountability. FULL RESPONSIBILITY. How relieving. No one else is involved.

Become BEST friends with the you that romanisiced, accepted, believed, it was ok and normal for you to be hit, spoken down to, controlled. There is no part of you that is to be resisted. These questions seem backwards cos ppl want to baby & console u, im giving victim blaming vibes, idc, we have to be real, for me it's the only way to heal.

Take a pen if you want or just answer aloud intuitively;

— viii —

Why you were drawn to this person, why they were so expressive and fascinating to you, so beautiful? Why did the darkness draw you in? Why you didn't want to leave when they became violent? Why were you not disinterested when they started to insult you? When they slowly changed?

Stay curious. Be honest.

What part of you was the vampire of the energy exchange? What were u learning from him? Getting from him? Feeling through him?

What were you trying to save? What could you see in him that he was trying to destroy? How did you relate to him? How did you feel when you could help him? Did it make you feel like a good person? Did sacrificing yourself feel good? Righteous? Did you get to see another side to yourself? When you would start to shout back, when you started to react and experience these crazy feelings? The energy it gave you or took away from you?

— ix —

If we can figure this all out, ALONE, with each other. We are fucking winning. we feel rejected anyways… time to create a new path.

I want you to take ur power back. To be really real with you, that man wasn't special. He wasn't different. A million men could Abuse you, manipulate you, hurt you & make u feel special and loved at the same time. Hurt people hurt people. The pattern will keep doing its thing…..

You have to break yourself open and see the flames, redirect the fire into your own health, ur dreams. Reparent yourself, forgive yourself. The past already is gone. You might have to kill your old self. It might take drastic changes, probably will. The ripples of the abuse will always be there, you have to ride the waves.

It's so gansta to heal, trust yourself, to smile. I always wanted to prove my pain but what a cool thing that they can't tell. I Thank God for everything Ive been through. Thank God what I wanted was refused and declined, that relationship was all I wanted at the time, and for my safety he was actually put away.

— x —

I Kept momentum of the chaos, just channeled in slightly more healthy ways each time. It all feels pointless, till you take more risks, redirect that energy, you've hit rock bottom now you need to do something whist you have nothing to lose.

I needed adventure. I needed to heal my faith in humanity, I needed to feel safe in my body, home on this planet. But in a new way, one I never experienced before. I still felt lonely, but I filled in the dark, shadow spaces of myself with curiosity, and new perceptions. I was curious, unaware of my on shadow, and him violently expressing his made me open to the world and myself more. I still give him too much credit, I think in this big playground of free will, these very realizations would have come from elsewhere. But that was my path.

I'll just say we need to see how precious and short life is, we don't need to keep our enemies THAT close.

Stay bad, stay safe, I am praying for youuu ♥ xoxo (gossip girl)

Phoebe Juanita
