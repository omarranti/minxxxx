Element 8 — Callout / Highlight Box
Colosseum tip: Book the Underground + Arena Floor tier — worth the extra £8 each. You'll avoid the main crowd entirely and get access to the hypogeum.
Amalfi ferry: Morning boats are least crowded. Last ferry back from Positano to Amalfi is 18:40 — do not miss it or it's a €60 taxi.
Currency: Italy is card-friendly everywhere except old-school trattorias and markets. Carry €100 cash at all times.
